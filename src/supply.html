Agri Waste Supply

Transforming agricultural liabilities into valuable energy assets directly from the source.

Sourcing at the Root

The foundation of our circular economy begins at the farm. India generates millions of tonnes of crop residue annually, much of which is traditionally burned in open fields, causing severe environmental and health crises. Our Agri Waste Supply service intercepts this cycle.

We work directly with local farmers, rural cooperatives, and agricultural mills to procure organic waste such as crop stubble, bagasse, husks, and stalks. By doing so, we clear the fields safely for the next harvest without the need for burning.

Key Benefits of our Supply Network:

  • Farmer Empowerment: We convert a disposal problem into a secondary, predictable income stream for the rural farming community.
  • Emission Prevention: By actively removing biomass from fields, we prevent tonnes of particulate matter and greenhouse gases from entering the atmosphere.
  • Consistent Feedstock: Establishing deep rural roots ensures our manufacturing facilities receive an uninterrupted supply of raw materials year-round.
