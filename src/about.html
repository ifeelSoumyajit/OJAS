About OJAS BIOFUEL.

Pioneering the transition to clean, sustainable industrial energy through indigenous circular economy solutions.

Our Vision & Mission

At OJAS BIOFUEL. LLP, we believe the future of industrial energy lies in the sustainable transformation of what others discard. We are committed to establishing a robust Circular Economy that benefits both local agriculture and heavy industry.

Our core mission revolves around solving two massive challenges simultaneously: reducing agricultural waste pollution and lowering the carbon footprint of manufacturing sectors.

  • 🌱 Zero-Waste Lifecycle: Transforming agri-residue into premium fuel.
  • ⚡ Carbon Neutrality: Helping industries transition away from fossil fuels.
  • 🤝 Farmer Empowerment: Creating secondary income streams for local agriculture.

Company History

Founded in Khadakwasla, Pune, OJAS BIOFUEL. LLP began with a simple but powerful idea: that local agricultural waste could be harnessed to power local industry.

Over the years, we have scaled our operations from foundational biomass trading to establishing comprehensive, sustainable fuel supply chains across the region.

  • Inception: Established headquarters in Pune with a focus on agri-waste procurement.
  • Expansion: Scaled up distribution networks for Biomass Briquettes and Pellets.
  • Today: A trusted partner for industries seeking high-efficiency, carbon-neutral industrial fuel solutions.
