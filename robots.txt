Since we have already knocked out a huge chunk of Technical SEO (HTTPS, fast WebP images, clean routing, caching, and the `sitemap.xml`), here is the strategic roadmap to fully optimize Ojas Biofuel for search engines.

### Phase 1: On-Page Fundamentals

These are the invisible tags in your HTML that dictate how the site appears on a Google Search results page.

* **Unique Meta Descriptions:** Every single page needs a unique, compelling `<meta name="description" content="...">` tag inside the `<head>`. Keep them under 160 characters.
* *Example for `/briquettes`:* `<meta name="description" content="Ojas Biofuel manufactures high-calorific biomass briquettes in Pune. Replace coal and furnace oil with our clean, sustainable, automation-ready green energy.">`


* **Title Tag Optimization:** Ensure every `<title>` includes high-value keywords, not just the page name. (e.g., `<title>Industrial Biomass Pellets Manufacturer | OJAS BIOFUEL</title>`).
* **Image Alt Text:** Search engines cannot "see" your `.webp` images. You must add descriptive `alt="..."` tags to every `<img>` element in the `/public/` folder, using relevant keywords (e.g., `alt="Bulk industrial biomass briquettes for manufacturing furnaces"`).

### Phase 2: Technical Additions

A few small files will give search engine crawlers explicit instructions on how to read your site.

* **Create a `robots.txt` File:** Drop a simple text file named `robots.txt` in your root folder (next to `index.html`) to point Google directly to your sitemap:
```text

```



User-agent: *
Allow: /
Sitemap: https://ojasbiofuel.in/sitemap.xml

```
*   **Implement Schema Markup (JSON-LD):** This is a snippet of code placed in the `<head>` of your `index.html` that explicitly tells Google this is a "Local Business" or "Organization." It is how you get rich snippets and knowledge panels in search results.

### Phase 3: Local & Off-Page Strategy
Since biofuel distribution relies heavily on regional logistics, local SEO is going to be Ojas Biofuel's primary driver of leads.

*   **Google Search Console:** Go to Search Console immediately, verify ownership of `ojasbiofuel.in`, and submit your `sitemap.xml`. This forces Google to index the new domain.
*   **Google Business Profile Optimization:** Use the 750-character description we drafted earlier. Add the specific products (Briquettes, Pellets, Industrial Fuel) to the profile's "Products" tab, and link the "Buy/Order" buttons directly to your clean URLs like `[https://ojasbiofuel.in/order](https://ojasbiofuel.in/order)`.
*   **Local Citations:** Ensure the exact Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP) listed on the website's `/contact` page matches the Google Business Profile and any regional business directories exactly.
```
