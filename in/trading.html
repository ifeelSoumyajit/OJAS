Biomass Trading

Bridging the gap between rural aggregators and industrial energy demands with reliability and scale.

Reliable Market Linkage

The raw biomass market in India is highly fragmented, localized, and historically informal. Ojas Biofuel acts as a stabilizing anchor in this market through our professional Biomass Trading operations.

We procure, assess, and trade bulk quantities of raw and semi-processed biomass. Our deep understanding of seasonal crop cycles and regional availability allows us to balance supply and demand efficiently, ensuring that bio-energy processing plants and direct-firing industries have access to the materials they need, exactly when they need them.

Our Trading Standards:

  • Quality Assurance: Every batch is rigorously tested for moisture, ash content, and calorific value to ensure maximum energy efficiency for the end-user.
  • Price Stability: By managing large-scale procurement and long-term contracts, we buffer our industrial clients against wild seasonal price fluctuations.
  • Logistical Efficiency: Our strategic network of aggregation hubs minimizes transit times and prevents the degradation of organic materials during transport.
